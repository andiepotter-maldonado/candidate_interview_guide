Glossary
Top 5 Industry Terms to Know

To help you feel prepared, here are key terms you'll hear in conversations at Mutinex.

1.

MMM (Marketing Mix Modelling)

A statistical method used to measure the impact of various marketing inputs (TV, digital, OOH, etc.) on sales or other business outcomes. Mutinex is reinventing how MMM is delivered ‚Äî as always-on SaaS instead of static consulting projects.

2.

MROI (Marketing Return on Investment)

The key metric that quantifies the revenue generated for every dollar spent on marketing. GrowthOS helps teams orient conversations around MROI to identify growth opportunities.

3.

Adstock

The prolonged effect of advertising on consumer behaviour after exposure. GrowthOS uses adstock analysis to help optimise campaign flighting and timing for maximum effectiveness.

4.

Incrementality

Measuring whether a marketing activity drove additional business outcomes that wouldn't have happened otherwise. This is the gold standard for understanding true marketing impact.

5.

Foundation Model

An AI model trained on broad data that can be adapted for specific tasks. Mutinex's Theseus AI is a generalised growth foundation model that learns across all customers to deliver better, faster insights.
